Introduction

Incepted during the year 2013,Sri Amman Textiles is a pioneer in manufacture and export of cotton grey fabrics, in India. Our company is managed by professionals like textile technicians and engineers to get best performance, quality and customer satisfaction. Our product is well accepted by readymade garment manufacturers and home furnishing manufacturers in domestic as well as international markets. We buy good quality yarn from reputed spinning mills after testing it in-house laboratory..

All our employees are well trained in their work. Our machines are well maintained under preventive maintenance system. We concentrate on cotton fabrics since the entire world is turning towards cotton, the natural fibre.

Cotton and Rayon fabric manufacturer

Sri Amman Textiles, is reckoned in the industry of textiles for offering quality based fabrics. The Cotton and Rayon fabric offered by us are high count and high density fabric. We also have the facility to offer solutions in bulk quantity as per the specifications of our clients.

We are a renowned manufacturer and supplier of Cotton and Rayon Fabrics that are offered at cost-effective rates. Our range is available in different sizes and designs as per the demands of the clients. These fabrics are widely appreciated for their strength and optimum quality. These are delivered to the clients within the agreed deadline.

We are manufacturing Cotton and Rayon fabrics of 20 counts to 80 counts.We are selling our fabrics in all over India and exporting around the globe.

Our Services

We are a one-stop destination and the perfect place for purchasing excellence assured Yarns , Cotton and Rayon Fabrics.We do Warping and Sizing with our own yarn and deliver to customer.
We are known for providing a qualitative array of products and thus our entire range is strictly checked against varied quality parameters.

Warping

Sizing

Weaving
