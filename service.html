About Us

SRI AMMAN GROUPS

Sri Amman Sizing and Weaving Mills was established in the year 2013. The unit was formed by six partners Mr.S.N Nithyanandhan,Mr.S.N.Swaminathan,Mr.A.Kalichamy,Mr.P.Gokul,Mr.K.Manojkumar and Ms.M.Suchittra.All the partners are directly involved in production and marketing of fabrics.

We are having our own spinning and karlmayer sizing units for the supply of good quality fabrics.

MANUFACTURING DEPARTMENT

Mr.K.Manojkumar(Executive Director) is taking care of entire manufacturing department.He is having adequate technical knowledge and is supported by well experienced team of technicians.They are ensuring production of good quality fabrics.

MARKETING DEPARTMENT

Headed by our Managing partners Mr.S.N.Nithyanandhan(Joint Managing Director) and Mr.P.Gokul(Managing Director).We are manufacturing Cotton and Rayon fabrics of 20 counts to 80 counts.We are selling our fabric in overall India and Exporting to all around the Global Nations.

QUALITY CONTROL

We are ensuring three stages of quality control:

STAGE 1

First stage of quality control is done by our,technical Manager at the looms itself.

STAGE 2

Second stage of quality Control is done manually through ultra light by our experienced staffs.

STAGE 3

Third stage of qulity control ensures the packing section for the export quality of fabrics.
