Love Our Works

We are highly quality conscious and have stringent quality control systems where our experienced and professional staff keeps vigilance to obtain best results. To achieve high quality standards, we continuously invest in up gradation of skills, revision and improvement in our processes & systems and testing equipments. We buy quality, make quality & sell quality. In our company, quality is our first priority.We ensure that we work in an Eco-friendly environment.

Every finished product arrives in the customer hand with the SRI AMMAN SIZING AND WEAVING MILLS personal guarantee of satisfaction. In order to achieve this guarantee, the quality control process starts right from selection of raw materials. The company has independent Quality Assurance team that conducts inline audits and monitors the performance of each department. To assure quality in the final product, process control methods are used in every step of the production.
